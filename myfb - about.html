ABOUT US

We are Bonbon Clouds, a pastry shop that offers a variety of desserts that will satisfy your cravings and will make you go cloudy.

Pastries Inspiration From Around The World

Our pastry chef travelled throughout the world to learn and understand the different countries' pastries. We want to deliver freshness to our customers at the same time new and exciting products. We want our customers to experience the world even without living the country through our pastries. Our pastries and imagination go well with each other, especially in dreaming of travelling the world.

What do we offer?

Bonbon Cloud offers various desserts that satisfy you sweet cravings. You can choose on our cupcakes, cookies, cakes, and more. We want you to enjoy pastries from all around the world. We want you to have the freshest and sweetest desserts that you'll ever have. It is our goal to give you the best experience in this industy.

Why choose us?

Our products are made from fresh and organic ingredients that you can enjoy while having a cup of coffee or maybe anything you would like. We also offer customized or personalized pastries according to your taste. Our products varies from local desserts that's from your childhood to foreign desserts that you haven't tasted before.

Charities and Donations

Bonbon Cloud believes that everyone deserves a cloud nine and sweet life. Our business donates 50% of the net profit to local charities. It is our mission to bring a better world to the people in need. We want to have a society that helps and establishes good in everything.

Mission

Our Mission is to help bakers and other local pastry owners in promoting this industry. We are here to offer people a wide selection of desserts that is new and never been seen before. It is our goal to showcase the beauty and delightfulness of sweet products around the world. It is also our ambition that everyone will experience a gratifying society.

Vision

To serve customers happiness through fresh and pure pastry products while also promoting the beauty in Pastry Industry.

To establish a well-known pastry shop that brings quality products, services, and greatest value to our customers.
